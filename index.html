The Cottage

Completely rebuilt in 2023.
Nothing left to do but relax.

Peaceful and serene, this spacious three-bedroom cottage sits on a rare level lot, set well back from the water beneath towering white pines — eighteen minutes from Bracebridge on the beautiful Muskoka River.

Rebuilt to the highest standards (with permits) in 2023, everything here is new — and it's a fully winterized, four-season home you can enjoy year-round. The great room's vaulted ceiling and wall of windows pull the river indoors, with plenty of room for all of your family and friends.

Mornings begin with coffee on the 14′ × 20′ deck-dock at the water's edge; evenings wind down with cocktails amid the setting sun. In winter, the setting is equally beautiful — perhaps more so.
